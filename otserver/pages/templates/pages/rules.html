Server Rules

Breaking rules can lead to mutes, jails, or bans. Ignorance is not an excuse.

  1. Respect & Conduct
    • No hate speech, harassment, or threats.
    • No real-money trading (RMT) outside the official Bazaar/Store.
  2. Cheating & Automation
    • No bots, macroing, or illegal software. Multi-clienting only as allowed by server policy.
    • No packet editing or exploiting bugs. Report bugs immediately.
  3. Names & Impersonation
    • No offensive or misleading names.
    • No pretending to be staff.
  4. Chat & Advertising
    • Use trade channels for buying/selling. Spam and flooding are not allowed.
    • External server ads are forbidden.
  5. Gameplay Fairness
    • No excessive griefing (luring, blocking, trapping) beyond PvP rules.
    • Respect house & spawn rules; don’t abuse map design.
  6. Account Security
    • Do not share accounts. You are responsible for your account and actions.
    • Enable any available security features; use a strong password.
  7. Staff & Appeals
    • Staff decisions are final. You may appeal respectfully via support.
    • Ticket falsehoods or witch-hunts can result in penalties.
