Ability Orbs

Power up your gear with Ability Orbs found inside two kinds of bags: Ability Orb Bag and Superior Ability Orb Bag. Each bag grants one random orb that adds a permanent bonus to a specific item slot — as shown in the orb’s description.

How To Obtain

Ability Orb Bags are rare loot dropped by skulled monsters — special versions of creatures that spawn randomly during hunts.

  • Red-Skulled Creatures
    These enhanced monsters have an increased chance to drop a regular Ability Orb Bag.
    Examples include Dragons, Giant Spiders, and Heroes.
  • Black-Skulled Creatures
    Only elite bosses such as Dragon Lords, Demons, and Hydras can drop the Superior Ability Orb Bag. Lesser monsters, even if black-skulled, will still only drop the regular version.

Skulled monsters appear at random and are marked by glowing skull icons above their heads. The stronger the skull color, the tougher the battle — and the better the loot chances.

How It Works

  1. Use a bag from your inventory:
    • Ability Orb Bag – standard bonus range.
    • Superior Ability Orb Bag – higher tier bonuses.
  2. You’ll receive one Ability Orb with:
    • Bonus type (e.g., Max Health, Sword Skill, Critical Chance).
    • Amount – the rolled value within that bag’s range.
    • Item – the equipment slot affected (e.g., Helmet, Axe, Bow).
  3. The orb’s description clearly shows its details:
Bonus: max health
Amount: 7
Item: Helmet

Note: Weapon-skill orbs (Axe, Sword, Club, or Distance) only target armor slots and the related weapon type. Other bonuses can land on any armor or weapon slot.

Tips

  • Farm red-skulled monsters for consistent Ability Orb Bag drops.
  • Hunt elite bosses (Dragon Lords, Demons, Hydras) for Superior bags.
  • Stack similar orbs to specialize your build — offense, defense, or speed.
